# WhatsApp Templates

WhatsApp Business API templates are pre-approved message formats required for sending notifications, marketing messages, and other business communications to customers. These templates must be approved by Meta before they can be used in campaigns.

<Info>
Templates are required for all outbound WhatsApp messages that are not responses to customer inquiries within the 24-hour customer service window.
</Info>

## Template Components

WhatsApp templates can include the following components:

### Header (Optional)
- **Text Header**: Static text or text with variables
- **Media Header**: Images, videos, or documents (not covered in this interface)

### Body (Required)
The main message content that customers will see. This is the only required component.

### Footer (Optional)
Small text that appears at the bottom of the message, typically used for disclaimers or additional context.

### Quick Reply Buttons (Optional)
Up to 3 buttons that customers can tap to respond quickly.

## Dynamic Variables

Templates support dynamic variables using the `{{1}}`, `{{2}}`, `{{3}}` syntax. These placeholders will be replaced with actual values when sending the message.

<Warning>
Variables must be numbered sequentially starting from 1. You cannot skip numbers (e.g., using `{{1}}` and `{{3}}` without `{{2}}`).
</Warning>

### Example with Variables

```
Header: Welcome {{1}}!
Body: Hi {{1}}, your appointment on {{2}} has been confirmed. Please arrive 15 minutes early at {{3}}.
Footer: Reply STOP to unsubscribe
```

When sending, you would provide:
- `{{1}}` = Customer name
- `{{2}}` = Appointment date
- `{{3}}` = Address

## Template Categories

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Marketing">
    Used for promotional messages, offers, and marketing campaigns. These templates have stricter approval requirements and quality scoring.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Utility">
    Used for account updates, order confirmations, and other transactional messages. Generally easier to get approved.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Authentication">
    Used for sending verification codes and authentication messages. Has the fastest approval process.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Template Status

### PENDING
The template has been submitted to Meta for review. This process typically takes 24-48 hours.

### APPROVED
The template has been approved and can be used in campaigns. Approved templates receive a quality score from Meta.

### REJECTED
The template was rejected by Meta. Check the rejection reason and modify the template accordingly before resubmitting.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Common Rejection Reasons">
    - Contains promotional content in a UTILITY template
    - Uses placeholder text like "Insert name here"
    - Violates WhatsApp Business Policy
    - Contains spelling or grammar errors
  </Step>
</Steps>

### PAUSED
The template was automatically paused due to low quality scores or policy violations.

## Creating Templates

<Steps>
  <Step title="Template Name">
    Choose a unique name using lowercase letters, numbers, and underscores only. Example: `appointment_reminder` or `order_confirmation_v2`
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Category">
    Choose the appropriate category based on your message purpose:
    - **MARKETING**: Promotions, offers, newsletters
    - **UTILITY**: Notifications, confirmations, updates  
    - **AUTHENTICATION**: OTP codes, login verification
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Choose Language">
    Select the language for your template. You can create multiple templates in different languages with the same name.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compose Message">
    Write your message components:
    - Header (optional): Keep it short and engaging
    - Body (required): Clear, concise message with variables if needed
    - Footer (optional): Disclaimers, unsubscribe info, etc.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add Quick Reply Buttons (Optional)">
    Add up to 3 buttons for quick customer responses like "Yes", "No", "More Info"
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Submit for Review">
    Submit your template to Meta for approval. The review process typically takes 24-48 hours.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Template Management

### Editing Templates
You can edit APPROVED templates, but any changes will require re-approval from Meta. The template status will change back to PENDING.

<Warning>
Editing an approved template will pause any active campaigns using that template until it's re-approved.
</Warning>

### Deleting Templates
Templates can be deleted, but this action cannot be undone. Make sure the template is not being used in any active campaigns.

### Quality Scores
Approved templates receive quality scores from Meta based on:
- Customer engagement rates
- Block and report rates
- Message relevance and usefulness

## Best Practices

<Steps>
  <Step title="Clear and Concise">
    Keep messages short and to the point. Customers should immediately understand the purpose.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Proper Category Selection">
    Choose the correct category. Using UTILITY for marketing messages will likely result in rejection.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Meaningful Variables">
    Use variables that add real value. Avoid placeholder text like "{{name}}" in your examples.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Professional Tone">
    Maintain a professional, helpful tone. Avoid excessive use of emojis or casual language.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Compliance">
    Ensure your templates comply with WhatsApp Business Policy and local regulations.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Variable Limits

Different template categories have different variable limits:

- **Header**: Up to 1 variable
- **Body**: Up to 10 variables  
- **Footer**: No variables allowed
- **Buttons**: No variables allowed

## Language Support

Templates can be created in multiple languages including:
- Spanish (es)
- English (en_US)
- Portuguese (pt_BR)
- French (fr)
- German (de)
- Italian (it)
- And many more...

<Info>
You can create the same template in multiple languages by using the same template name with different language codes.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

### Template Rejected
1. Review the rejection reason provided by Meta
2. Check for compliance with WhatsApp Business Policy
3. Ensure proper category selection
4. Fix any spelling or formatting issues
5. Resubmit the corrected template

### Variables Not Working
- Ensure variables are numbered sequentially ({{1}}, {{2}}, {{3}})
- Don't skip numbers in the sequence
- Variables are case-sensitive

### Approval Taking Too Long
- Standard approval time is 24-48 hours
- Authentication templates are usually approved faster
- Marketing templates may take longer due to stricter review
