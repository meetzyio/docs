Every Meetzy user—whether you're the account owner, an admin, or a team member—has access to a personal **Account settings** page. This is where you manage how you appear to your team and how you sign in, without needing to contact support.

<Info>
You can reach Account settings at any time from the user menu in the top-right corner of the dashboard.
</Info>

## What you can manage

The page is organized into three areas:

1. **Identity summary** — a quick glance at your name, email, and account role.
2. **Profile** — the fields your teammates (or your invoices) see.
3. **Sign-in email** — a secure, two-step flow to change the email you use to log in.

<Info>
The fields available in the **Profile** section depend on your role. The **account owner** manages billing-facing details (full name and company), while **admins and members** manage the details shown in the team directory (name and job title).
</Info>

## Identity summary

At the top of the page you'll see a card with:

- An avatar generated from your initials
- Your display name and sign-in email
- A role badge: **Account owner**, **Admin**, or **Member**

This is a read-only summary—use the sections below to make changes.

## Editing your profile

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Account owner">
    As the account owner, your profile fields represent your business identity across Meetzy, including anywhere your company details are referenced (such as invoices).

    - **Full name** — your name as the account owner (e.g. `Jane Smith`)
    - **Company** — your company name (e.g. `Acme Inc.`)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Update your details">
        Edit the **Full name** and/or **Company** fields.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save changes">
        Click **Save changes**. A confirmation message appears once your profile is updated.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Admin or Member">
    As an admin or member, your profile fields control how you appear in the **Team** member list to the rest of the account.

    - **Name** — your display name (e.g. `Jane Smith`)
    - **Job title** — your role or position (e.g. `Head of Sales`)

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Update your details">
        Edit the **Name** and/or **Job title** fields.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Save changes">
        Click **Save changes**. A confirmation message appears once your profile is updated.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Warning>
If saving fails, an inline error message will explain what went wrong. Double-check your entries and try again after a moment.
</Warning>

## Changing your sign-in email

Because your sign-in email is a sensitive credential, changing it requires confirming your current password first. This protects your account even if your session is left open on a shared device.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the change">
    In the **Sign-in email** section, click **Change email**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your current password">
    Enter your current account password and click **Continue**. This re-authenticates your session before allowing any change to your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your new email address">
    Once your password is verified, enter the new email address you'd like to use to sign in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm from your inbox">
    Submit the new address. You'll be prompted to check your inbox to confirm the change—your sign-in email won't update until you confirm it there.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
If your password confirmation expires before you finish entering the new email, you'll be asked to re-enter your password. This is a safety measure to make sure the person changing the email is still you.
</Info>

<Warning>
If the new email address is already associated with another Meetzy account, the change will be rejected. Choose a different address or contact support if you believe this is an error.
</Warning>

## Role differences at a glance

| Role | Profile fields | Can change sign-in email |
|---|---|---|
| Account owner | Full name, Company | Yes |
| Admin | Name, Job title | Yes |
| Member | Name, Job title | Yes |

<Info>
Your account role (owner, admin, or member) is assigned by your account administrator and cannot be changed from this page. Visit the **Team** page to view or manage roles if you have admin permissions.
</Info>
