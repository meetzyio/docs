## Overview

Basic Settings is the first section in the Advanced Editor where you configure the fundamental properties of your AI agent. These settings define how your agent sounds, which language it speaks, which AI model powers its intelligence, and the foundational components that shape its behavior.

## Agent Identification

### Agent Name

Give your agent a descriptive name that helps you identify it in your dashboard.

<Info>
Use clear naming conventions like "Customer Support - English" or "Sales Outbound - Spanish" to easily manage multiple agents.
</Info>

### Company Selection (Admin Only)

Administrators can assign agents to specific companies in the system.

<Warning>
Company assignment affects which resources and phone numbers are available to the agent.
</Warning>

## Phone Configuration

### Phone Numbers

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="New Agents">
    When creating a new agent, select from available phone numbers in your Twilio account:

    <Steps>
      <Step title="Select Twilio Account">
        Administrators can choose which Twilio account to use for the agent
      </Step>
      <Step title="Choose Phone Number">
        Pick an available phone number from the dropdown
      </Step>
      <Step title="Request More Numbers">
        Use the "Request more numbers" link if you need additional phone numbers
      </Step>
    </Steps>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Existing Agents">
    Phone numbers for existing agents are read-only and cannot be changed after creation.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Caller IDs (Client Numbers)

Configure client phone numbers for outbound calls:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Add Caller ID">
    Add client phone numbers that the agent can use when making outbound calls
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verification">
    Twilio will send a verification code to validate the phone number
  </Step>
  <Step title="Status Management">
    Monitor verification status and manage caller ID entries
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Caller IDs must be verified with Twilio before they can be used for outbound calls.
</Info>

  </Tab>

  </Tab>

## Voice Settings Parameters

When fine-tuning your agent's voice, consider these parameter effects:

| Parameter | Recommended Range | Impact |
|-----------|------------------|---------|
| Speed | 1.0x - 1.1x | Natural speaking pace for phone calls |
| Stability | 0.1 - 0.3 | Higher stability for consistent voice |
| Similarity Boost | 0.6 - 0.8 | Balances naturalness and clarity |

<Info>
**Default Recommendation**: Speed: 1.06x, Stability: 0.15, Similarity Boost: 0.65
</Info>

## Troubleshooting

<Warning>
**Model Changes**: Changing the AI model may affect your agent's behavior. Always test in the Playground after switching models.
</Warning>

<Warning>
**Phone Number Assignment**: Phone numbers cannot be changed after agent creation. Plan your phone number allocation carefully.
</Warning>

<Warning>
**Voice Compatibility**: Ensure selected voice supports your chosen language for optimal performance.
</Warning>
