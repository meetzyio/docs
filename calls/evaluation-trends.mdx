The Evaluation Trends feature provides comprehensive analytics on your agents' evaluation performance over time. This powerful tool helps you identify patterns, track improvements, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your agents' effectiveness.

## Accessing Evaluation Trends

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Agents">
    Go to your agents list from the main navigation menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select an Agent">
    Use the agent selector to choose the agent you want to analyze:
    - Click on the search field to browse available agents
    - Type to search by agent name
    - Select your desired agent from the dropdown
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Evaluations">
    Click the "View evaluations" button to access the trends dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the Metrics Dashboard

### Summary Cards

At the top of the dashboard, you'll find three key performance indicators:

1. **Total Evaluations** - The total number of evaluations performed in the selected period
2. **Global Success Rate** - The overall success percentage across all evaluations
3. **Total Calls Evaluated** - The number of individual calls that were evaluated

### Interactive Performance Charts

Each evaluation criterion has its own interactive chart showing:

## Tips for Effective Analysis

### Choosing the Right Time Frame

- **Short-term analysis (7-14 days)**: Great for immediate performance assessment and quick fixes
- **Medium-term analysis (30 days-3 months)**: Ideal for identifying trends and planning improvements
- **Long-term analysis (6+ months)**: Perfect for strategic planning and annual reviews

### Reading the Data

- **Focus on trends rather than individual data points** - Temporary dips are normal
- **Look for consistent patterns** - Sustained improvements or declines are more significant
- **Consider external factors** - Changes in agent configuration, training, or business conditions

### Taking Action

When you identify areas for improvement:

1. **Document findings** - Keep notes on what the data reveals
2. **Plan interventions** - Develop specific actions to address issues
3. **Monitor results** - Track whether changes lead to improvements
4. **Iterate and improve** - Continuously refine your approach based on data
