The Evaluation Trends feature provides comprehensive analytics on your agents' evaluation performance over time. This powerful tool helps you identify patterns, track improvements, and make data-driven decisions to optimize your agents' effectiveness through an intuitive dashboard with rich visualizations and detailed metrics.

## Accessing Evaluation Trends

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Agents">
    Go to your agents list from the main navigation menu
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select an Agent">
    Use the agent selector to choose the agent you want to analyze:
    - Click on the search field to browse available agents
    - Type to search by agent name
    - Select your desired agent from the dropdown
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Evaluations">
    Click the "View evaluations" button to access the comprehensive trends dashboard
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Dashboard Overview

The evaluation trends dashboard provides a complete view of your agent's performance through several key sections:

### Summary Metrics Cards

At the top of the dashboard, three key performance indicators provide instant insights:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Total Evaluations">
    Shows the complete count of evaluations performed during the selected time period. This metric helps you understand the volume of assessment activity.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Global Success Rate">
    Displays the overall success percentage across all evaluations, giving you a quick snapshot of overall performance quality.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Calls Evaluated">
    Indicates the total number of individual calls that underwent evaluation, helping you track coverage and assessment scope.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Info>
These summary cards automatically update based on your selected time range and provide real-time calculations from your filtered dataset.
</Info>

## Troubleshooting and Support

### Common Display Issues

- **Missing data**: Ensure evaluations were performed during the selected time period
- **Chart loading problems**: Refresh the page or try a different time range
- **Search not working**: Clear the search field and try again with different terms

### Data Accuracy

The dashboard reflects evaluation data in real-time, but consider:

- **Processing delays**: Recent evaluations may take a few minutes to appear
- **Time zone considerations**: Data is displayed in your account's configured time zone
- **Filter interactions**: Multiple filters may limit visible data
